Our goal

The proposal that we have accepted aims to create a platform to enhance learning of Machine Learning and AI topics in a classroom setting. This will save the teacher a lot of time spent grading students' work and will make the organization of assignments easier.

OpenUP

Project built under OpenUP guidelines

Inception

In this phase, the project vision, objectives and scopes are defined. The stakeholders are identified and their requirements and expectations are captured.

Elaboration

The requirements are analysed and refined to identify the core functionality of the system. The architecture is developed and a prototype of the system is built to validate the design.

Construction

The software components are developed and integrated into the system. The system is tested to ensure that it meets the requirements and specifications.

Transition

In this phase, the system is deployed to the product enviroment. The users are trained on the system and the system is transitioned into the maintenance phase.
